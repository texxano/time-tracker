texxano://checkin

Or right-click the QR code above and select "Save image as..."

📋 Instructions:

  • Download: Click "Download QR Code" to save the image
  • Print: Print the QR code at least 2x2 inches (5x5 cm) for easy scanning
  • Place: Position at entrance/exit or time clock area
  • Test: Click "Test Deep Link" on your mobile device to verify it works
  • Protect: Laminate or use plastic sleeve to protect the QR code

ℹ️ How it works:

  • Employee scans QR code with phone camera
  • Texxano app opens automatically (even if closed)
  • Welcome modal appears
  • Time tracking starts automatically
  • Employee can continue working
